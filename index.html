A Headline Here

A total of 3,810 awards were given between 1984 and 2004. National Geographic, The Washington Post, and The Associated Press were the top three award-winning organizations.

Freelance Photographers

Between 1984 and 2004, freelance photographers won the most awards. Not suprisingly, this number has increased since the beginning of the competition as full-time jobs (newspapers a primary employer) for photographers have decreased dramatically over the years.
